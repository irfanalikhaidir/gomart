Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?
+
Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You can cancel anytime.
Which marketplaces does Gomart connect to?
+
Gomart integrates with Shopee, Tokopedia, Lazada, TikTok Shop, WooCommerce, Shopify, and 15+ more platforms.
How does the AI assistant work?
+
Gumbo is trained on e-commerce data and connected to your store. It reads your sales history, inventory, and customer data to give personalized advice.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
+
Absolutely. You can change your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is my business data safe?
+
Yes. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Gomart is SOC 2 Type II certified and never sells your data to third parties.
How long does setup take?
+
Most sellers are fully connected and running within 5 minutes. Just sign up, connect your store, and Gomart starts analyzing your data immediately.
